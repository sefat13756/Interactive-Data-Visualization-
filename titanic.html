Project 2: Titanic Passenger List Dataset

Sefat E Rahman

Visualization 1: Bar Chart

This bar chart represents the total number of survived people based on class. To answer the question, how many people survived from each class, bar chart is the best choice. On mouse hover the exact number is shown.

Visualization 2: Scatter Plot

This scatterplot plots the people over 30 years of age. Primarily there are two colors based on the survival status of the plotted pessengers. On mouse hover, one ring is added on each circle. The color of the ring is based on the embarked status. The radius of the circle is changed based on the fare. The radius of the circle is higher if the fare is more than the minimum price. If we want to know the emabaekd,survival status of people over 30 years of old, then this scatterplot is the best to answer the question. On mouse hover, the name of each passenger is shown.

Visualization 3: Stacked Bar Chart

This group bar chart gives the survival idea of the pessengers of each class. This bar chart also shows the status of gender wise survived status. Y axis represents total count and x axis represents the class. In order to know the survival status of each class based on gender, this stacked bar chart is the perfect choice.

Visualization 4: Pie Chart

This pie chart groups the number of people based on age. This pie chart answers how many young, middle aged and senior citizens were there in Titanic.
